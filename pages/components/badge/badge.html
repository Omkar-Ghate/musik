    Badge

    Badges are being used to display a notification count or status information

    Status Badges

    We have 2 types of status badges that can be integrated with Avatars. You can show colors to show the online status of user. And to show cart or notification count, you can use number badge.

    Status Badges with Icons

    To show cart or notification count, you can use the status badge with icons.

    Text Badges

    We have 2 types of text badges that can be integrated with text. Badge with rounded edges and square edges. Font size of badge text is in em. It will change as per the parent element's font-size.
